Principal Investigator

Dr. Melanie C. Dispenza

Melanie Dispenza is an Assistant Professor of Medicine in the Division of Allergy and Clinical Immunology at the Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine. She is a translational physician-scientist whose research focuses on mechanisms, biomarkers, and prevention of immediate hypersensitivity reactions.
